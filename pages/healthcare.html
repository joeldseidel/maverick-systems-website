We're working on a more efficient medical supply chain

Maverick SCMS is a system designed specifically for improving the visibility and analytic capabilities of the medical device supply chain. By making use of data from the FDA, SCMS creates a profile for every device subject to the FDA GUDID program and provides an interface for wholesalers, manufacturers, and hospital procurement organizations to track the progress of a medical device through the supply chain.

Our system provides a user friendly interface to track medical devices, view device properties, and work towards a better understanding of the supply chain as whole to identify areas for increased efficiency and safety. Our goal is to ensure the integrity and available of supply chain data to all entities involved. SCMS makes this possible on a scale that was previously unprecedented.

Unlike other inventory and supply chain management systems, Maverick SCMS is built to work with existing data systems and compliment proprietary inventory management systems. SCMS, instead of replacing these systems, bridges the gaps between the systems of several supply chain stakeholders and provides data continuity, availability, and ensured integrity to the data passing between.
