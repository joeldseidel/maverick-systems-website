Maverick Supply Chain Management System

A comprehensive medical device tracking and supply chain management system built to increase visibility and reduce inefficiencies

The medical device supply chain is a complex and dynamic environment with countless links. Maverick Supply Chain Management System (SCMS) provides a big-picture perspective to the supply chain to facilitate reducing inefficiencies.

The comprehensive tracking, thorough management, and powerful analytics tools with the SCMS allow device manfacturers, wholesalers, distributors, and procurement organizations to follow a medical device from manufacturer to end user, all in one, easy to use system.

Supply chain management is all about visibility and Maverick SCMS provides a dynamic and revolutionary interface for viewing data about a device, where it has been, and planning where it is going. From one medical device to thousands of pallets worth, Maverick can handle it all.

Our development team is excited about and driven to produce powerful, effective, and innovative tools to improve our user's supply chains improve the framework of the medical industry. Frequent, meaningful updates provide new functionality and expand upon Maverick SCMS's extensive assembly of features.
